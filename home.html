Women Who Code

Women have been instrumental in the development of computer software from the beginning.
Here are a few of the women who have made important contributions.

Augusta Ada

  • Mathematician & Writer
  • One of the first computer programmers
  • One of the first to publish articles describing computing machines as more than number crunchers.
  • Computer Program Language Ada is named after her
  • "Ada Lovelace Day" is an annual event celebrated in mid-October to raise profile of women in STEM
